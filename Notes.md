  - creational (5)
  - structural (7)

  - factory method (C)
  - adapter (S)
  - composite (S)
  - decorator (S)
  - observer (B)
  - strategy (B)

- pattern = name + problem + solution + trade-offs
- 2 object-oriented design principles
